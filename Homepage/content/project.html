About the Project

Every Cloud Requires a Secure Lining

This web application serves as an educational space for those interested in accumulating knowledge and hands on experience administrating a cloud based system in Amazon Web Services.

Cloud technology is becoming an increasingly prevalent and important technology in today's world of computing, which dictates an increase in the necessity for best practices in terms of securing information.

The evolution of malware and exploitation techniques coupled with an ever increasing amount of PII hosted on the web, it is our duty and responsibility in the tech community to stay up to date with ways to discover, remediate and prevent these threats.
